RSVP Fast Read is a high-performance reading tool designed for efficiency and privacy. Using the Rapid Serial Visual Presentation (RSVP) technique, it flashes text one word at a time, allowing you to read significantly faster by eliminating eye movement.

Key Features:
- Speed Control: Read from 100 to 1000 words per minute.
- Optimal Recognition Point (ORP): Colored visual anchor point mathematically centered to keep your focus sharp.
- Bionic Reading Mode: Bolded word prefixes to assist with ocular anchoring.
- Advanced Views: Split-view for simultaneous RSVP and context scrolling, or Contextual Hybrid overlay.
- Smart Navigation: Tap-to-resume from any word, chapter selection, and 10-word rewind/skip.
- Document Support: Extract text from PDF, EPUB, HTML, and TXT files locally.
- Productivity: Track your daily reading progress and set focus timers.
- 100% Privacy: Strictly offline. No internet permissions, no trackers, and no data collection.

Improve your reading speed and focus with a tool built for freedom and transparency.
